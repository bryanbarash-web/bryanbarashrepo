Loyalty & Marketing Dashboard

The new Loyalty and Marketing Dashboard serves as your marketing mission control, providing a unified view of customer retention and campaign ROI. Move beyond basic stats to track total points liability in real time, compare membership tiers, and see exactly which automated workflows, such as Welcome Series and Win-Back campaigns, are driving the most revenue. The e-commerce funnel provides a clear look at the entire shopper journey from the first click to the final purchase across both your mobile app and web menu.

Mobile Responsive Reporting

Keep an eye on your business from anywhere. We have redesigned our highest-priority reports, including the Sales Overview, Closing Report, and Inventory Overview, to be fully mobile-responsive. Charts, tables, and interactive filters now adapt to your screen, providing clear visibility into your store's performance without horizontal scrolling or zooming.

E-Commerce
Customer Data

Holistic Customer Order History

Close the gap between your physical and digital storefronts. Every in-store purchase is now automatically reflected in a customer's unified purchase history, allowing them to see their complete transaction record whether they shop online, on the app, or at the register. By centralizing this data, you provide shoppers with a single, clear view of their favorite products and total spend across every channel.

Shopping Experience

Express Re-Ordering

Simplify the shopping journey for your loyal customers with Express Re-Ordering. Instead of hunting down individual items one by one, shoppers can now instantly rebuild an entire previous order and add it to their cart in a single action. This removes the friction of starting from scratch and ensures your repeat customers can get exactly what they love without the manual effort of rebuilding their cart.

Product Data

Total Terpenes on Product Cards & Details Pages

Help your shoppers find exactly what they are looking for by bringing lab-verified terpene data directly to your menu. You can now display the Total Terpenes percentage on both product cards and Product Detail Pages. This data syncs automatically from your Dutchie POS lab results, giving flavor-focused consumers the transparency they need to shop by effect and aroma rather than just potency alone.

Delivery
Delivery Operations

Route Optimization for Dynamic Delivery

Streamline your delivery operations with a deep, bidirectional integration between Onfleet and Dutchie. This update enables real-time route synchronization and automated manifest updates while maintaining full Metrc compliance. Orders now intelligently auto-assign to the nearest driver based on proximity, inventory, and current workload, ensuring your fleet operates at peak efficiency rather than just following chronological order.

Delivery Pricing

Separate Delivery Fees for ASAP vs. Scheduled Delivery

Tailor your delivery service to match customer demand. You can now set separate delivery fees for "ASAP" orders versus "Scheduled" windows, allowing you to charge a premium for immediate service or offer incentives for pre-planned orders.
