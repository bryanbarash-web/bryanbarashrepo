Merchandising

Menu Tags & Limited Categories

Transform your menu into a dynamic sales engine by automating the way you clear aging inventory. Using a combination of Menu Tags and Smart Tags, you can now create Limited Categories, also known as Outlet Menus, that automatically feature products based on specific rules like approaching expiration dates or low stock levels. These categories include native countdown timers on the product page and in the cart to create urgency and drive immediate conversions for your clearance items.

Merchandising > Smart Tags → Collections > Custom Categories > Limited Category
Marketing AI ▶ Loom

Smart Sending

Maximize your campaign impact by reaching customers exactly when they are most likely to shop. Our new AI-driven Smart Sending uses individual purchase history to predict each customer's unique "shopping window" and automatically delivers your SMS or email within two hours of that peak time. Instead of a one-size-fits-all blast, your messages deploy throughout the day to match your customers' habits, increasing open rates and driving more immediate sales.

Marketing > Campaigns > Toggle Smart Sending
Marketing

Dynamic Content Blocks

Boost conversions by adding dynamic content blocks to your marketing messages. Drag and drop personalized "Buy It Again" recommendations, segment-specific discounts, and real-time loyalty data directly into your campaigns. These blocks automatically show each customer their available rewards and the exact spend needed to reach the next tier, making every message feel tailored to their shopping habits.

Marketing > Email Gallery > Drag Block

Holistic Customer Order History

In-store orders are now attached to customer profiles at POS, ensuring every walk-in visit is captured in the customer's unified history. This allows for more accurate segmenting and ensures budtenders have the full context of a customer's lifetime value the moment they check in.

POS > Customer Profile
Shopping Experience

One-Click Buy It Again

Customers can instantly re-order their favorite products directly from their profile or previous order history without navigating the full menu. Removes checkout friction, increases order frequency, and rewards repeat customers with a faster experience.

Customer Profile > Order History

Route Optimization for Dynamic Delivery

Deep bidirectional integration between Onfleet and Dutchie enables real-time route synchronization and automated manifest updates while maintaining full Metrc compliance. Orders intelligently auto-assign to the nearest driver based on proximity, inventory, and current workload.
