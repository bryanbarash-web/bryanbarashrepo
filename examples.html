What's New

Merchandising & Automation

Feature 01

Smart Tags

Rules-based automation lets you tag inventory items based on lab results, pricing trends, or expiration dates—without manual intervention. Once tagged, items auto-trigger relevant discounts and surface in targeted menu collections, keeping your displays fresh and your margins protected. Configure everything directly in the Inventory tab.

Inventory Automation
Feature 02

Brand Portal V2

Brands can now create and manage their own product catalogs—complete with verified imagery and standardized descriptions—distributed across the Dutchie network. Retailers retain full menu visibility control, ensuring only approved content reaches their storefronts. The result: consistent, high-quality product presentation with zero overhead on your end.

Brand Network Catalog

Retailers retain full menu visibility control—every brand asset, every product description, every image goes through your approval before it ever reaches a customer.

Dutchie Product Team — Brand Portal V2
Feature 03

Exclusive Channel Offers

Create platform-specific promotions—Mobile-Only deals, Kiosk-Only specials—using intelligent segment logic. Drive channel adoption by rewarding customers who engage through your preferred touchpoints. Configure under Marketing › Discounts › Order Sources.

Marketing Segments
Feature 04

Event-Based Lead Generation

Deploy QR codes at pop-ups and festivals for instant loyalty enrollment and real-time revenue attribution. Every scan captures a new customer, triggers a rewards onboarding flow, and feeds directly into your analytics. Setup in Marketing › Events.

Marketing Events
02
Growth & Retention

Segments, Comms & Loyalty

Feature 05

Comms Preferences

Customers and budtenders can now manage their own opt-in statuses and notification preferences—SMS, Email, and Push—directly at the POS. Transparent consent management builds trust, reduces churn from over-messaging, and keeps your communications compliant and customer-driven.

POS Compliance
Feature 06

Enhanced Segment Attributes

30+ new filtering options now available in Customers › Segments—including loyalty status, spend behavior, purchase patterns, and channel preferences. Build hyper-targeted audiences that respond to the campaigns you send.

Segments 30+ Filters
Feature 07

Custom Segment Badges at Register

Visual indicators—custom icons and color-coded badges—instantly surface customer segments at the register. VIPs, medical patients, and high-value regulars are recognized on sight, enabling budtenders to deliver a tailored service experience every time.

POS Experience

With 30+ new segment attributes, you can finally build audiences that reflect how your customers actually behave—not just what they bought last week.

Dutchie Product Team — Enhanced Segments
Feature 08

Employee Rewards & Guardrails

Create dedicated employee segments with structured incentive programs—and safeguard your margins with built-in guardrails that prevent discount stacking. Recognize and reward your team without opening the door to unintended promotional abuse. Configure in Marketing › Loyalty › Settings.

Loyalty Employee Guardrails
03
E-Commerce & Mobile

Digital Storefront & Infrastructure

Feature 09

Multi-Image Product Cards

Product cards on your menu now support full image galleries. Customers scroll through multiple photos—packaging, product close-ups, lifestyle imagery—directly within the menu view, reducing uncertainty and increasing add-to-cart confidence.

E-Commerce Gallery
Feature 10

Custom Mobile Menu

A drag-and-drop menu builder designed for mobile. Highlight featured brands, reorder categories, surface loyalty links, and create a menu experience that reflects your store's identity—no code required, updates live instantly.

Mobile Builder
Feature 11 — Infrastructure

AWS Database Migration

In partnership with AWS, we've rebuilt Dutchie's core transaction infrastructure from the ground up. The migration delivers meaningfully faster checkout performance under peak load, automatic horizontal scaling during high-traffic periods, and enhanced redundancy across all store types—whether you run a single location or a multi-state operation. This is the foundation everything else is built on.

AWS Partnership Core Infrastructure Auto-Scaling
