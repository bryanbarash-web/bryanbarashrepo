What's New
01
Inventory New 01 / 11

Smart Tags

Automate the way your inventory is organized and promoted with intelligent, rules-based tagging. Smart Tags analyze your products in real time and apply dynamic labels based on your exact criteria — no manual updates required.

Rules-based automation that tags your inventory by lab results, pricing trends, or expiration dates — instantly triggering the right discounts and menu collections.

  • Lab Result Triggers: Auto-tag products based on THC/CBD thresholds or terpene profiles from lab data
  • Pricing Intelligence: Tag items dynamically as pricing approaches certain thresholds or cost margins change
  • Expiration Automation: Date-proximity rules auto-apply clearance tags and trigger discount workflows
  • Menu Collections: Tags instantly surface the right products in the right menu collections without manual curation
Setup
Dutchie Back Office → Inventory Tab
02
Brand Portal New 02 / 11

Brand Portal V2

Brands and retailers now share a single source of truth for product content across the entire Dutchie network. Brand Portal V2 gives brands direct control over how their products appear — while giving retailers the final say on their menu.

  • Verified Product Catalogs: Brands create and manage authoritative catalogs with approved imagery and descriptions
  • Network-Wide Syndication: Content syncs across all participating retailers automatically
  • Retailer Visibility Control: Retailers retain full authority over which products appear on their menu and how
  • Brand-Verified Imagery: High-quality, consistent product images replace inconsistent retailer uploads

Consistent brand presence across every retailer in the Dutchie network — without sacrificing retailer control.

03
Marketing E-Commerce New 03 / 11

Exclusive Channel Offers

Drive customers to specific channels with platform-exclusive promotions. Using Dutchie's segment logic, you can now create offers that are only redeemable through a specific touchpoint — rewarding customers who shop where you want them to shop.

  • Mobile-Only Deals: Exclusive promotions locked to the mobile app experience to drive app adoption
  • Kiosk-Only Offers: In-store kiosk discounts that streamline checkout and reduce budtender load
  • Segment Logic Integration: Layer channel conditions on top of existing customer segment rules
  • Order Source Targeting: Precise targeting by any configured order source across your stack
Setup
Marketing → Discounts → Order Sources
04
Marketing Loyalty New 04 / 11

Event-Based Lead Generation

Turn every pop-up, festival, or community event into a measurable loyalty acquisition channel. Generate QR codes that connect real-world moments to your digital rewards program — instantly.

Every handshake at a festival becomes a trackable loyalty enrollment. QR codes bridge the physical event to your digital rewards ecosystem in seconds.

  • QR Code Generation: Create event-specific QR codes directly from the Marketing dashboard
  • Instant Rewards Enrollment: Customers scan and join your loyalty program on the spot, no friction
  • Revenue Attribution: Track which events, codes, and campaigns drive actual purchase behavior
  • Campaign Performance: Full analytics on enrollments, first purchases, and lifetime value per event
Setup
Marketing → Events

POS Comms 05 / 11

Comms Preferences

Give customers and budtenders full visibility and control over communication preferences right at the point of sale. Manage opt-in statuses across every channel during the natural flow of a transaction.

  • Multi-Channel Control: Manage SMS, Email, and Push notification preferences in one unified view
  • At-Register Workflow: Budtenders can update preferences during checkout without disrupting the customer experience
  • Customer Self-Service: Customers can review and update their own preferences on demand
  • Compliance-Ready: Preference records update instantly, supporting regulatory opt-in/opt-out requirements
06
Segments Enhanced 06 / 11

Enhanced Segment Attributes

Build more precise, high-converting audiences with a dramatically expanded filtering toolkit. 30+ new attributes let you slice your customer base on dimensions that actually predict behavior.

30+ new filtering dimensions. Build segments so precise you can target customers who prefer mobile, spend above a threshold, and haven't visited in 45 days — all at once.

  • Loyalty Status: Tier membership, points balance, redemption history, and program engagement
  • Spend Behavior: Average order value, total lifetime spend, spend velocity, and cart composition
  • Purchase Patterns: Category affinity, brand loyalty, purchase frequency, and recency windows
  • Channel Preferences: Preferred ordering channel, device type, and pickup vs. delivery history
07
POS Segments 07 / 11

Custom Segment Badges at Register

Empower your budtenders with instant, visual customer intelligence the moment a transaction begins. Custom badges surface the context they need to deliver a personalized, high-value experience — without interrupting their flow.

  • Visual Indicators: Custom icons and color-coded badges appear on customer profiles at register
  • VIP Recognition: Instantly identify high-value customers for elevated service treatment
  • Medical Patient Flags: Clearly surface medical patient status to ensure compliant, appropriate interactions
  • Custom Segment Labels: Configure any segment to display with its own badge, color, and icon

Budtenders know who they're talking to before they say a word. Visual badges turn customer data into immediate, actionable context at the point of sale.

08
Loyalty POS 08 / 11

Employee Rewards & Guardrails

Motivate your team with structured incentive programs while maintaining tight control over how discounts are applied. Employee Rewards & Guardrails creates a system where staff are rewarded for performance — without the risk of discount abuse.

  • Employee Segments: Create dedicated loyalty tiers and reward structures specifically for staff
  • Incentive Programs: Configure performance-based rewards tied to sales metrics, upsells, or other KPIs
  • Discount Stacking Prevention: Built-in guardrails block unauthorized combination of employee and customer discounts
  • Configurable Controls: Define exactly which discount combinations are permissible and which are blocked
Setup
Marketing → Loyalty → Settings

E-Commerce & Mobile
09
E-Commerce Mobile 09 / 11

Multi-Image Product Cards

Let your products tell a richer visual story. Multi-Image Product Cards bring a gallery experience to your menu — customers can now scroll through multiple product images directly from the menu view, without navigating away.

  • Inline Gallery: Swipeable image galleries embedded directly in the product card on menu pages
  • Multiple Perspectives: Showcase packaging, product close-ups, lifestyle imagery, and lab certificates
  • Mobile-Optimized: Touch-friendly swipe gestures that feel native on iOS and Android
  • Seamless UX: Gallery browsing without leaving the menu — maintaining customer purchase momentum

More images, fewer abandoned carts. Visual confidence at the menu level means customers click add-to-cart instead of second-guessing their selection.

10
Mobile E-Commerce 10 / 11

Custom Mobile Menu

Your mobile menu should reflect your brand — not a default template. The new drag-and-drop menu builder gives you full creative control over the mobile shopping experience, with no code required.

  • Drag-and-Drop Builder: Intuitive visual editor for rearranging menu sections, categories, and featured content
  • Brand Highlighting: Prominently feature partner brands or your own private-label products in custom-placed modules
  • Loyalty Integration Links: Embed deep links to loyalty dashboards, point balances, and reward redemption flows
  • Section Customization: Rename, reorder, show, or hide any menu section to match your merchandising strategy

A mobile menu built around your brand strategy, not a generic template. Drag, drop, and deploy — your store, your order.
